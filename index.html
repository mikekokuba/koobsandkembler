Wedding Details

We will be saying "I do" at the Grand Rapids Downtown Market, conveniently located… downtown! The market hosts several local vendors on the first floor, a greenhouse, demo kitchens and banquet hall on the second and a fantastic outdoor farmers market during the summer months.

The ceremony will begin at 5pm in the Greenhouse. Yes, a working greenhouse! We know what you're thinking, and yes- we're crazy. Cross your fingers for mild weather but we've got a back-up plan just in case our Michigan weather gets a little out of hand. A cocktail hour will be held immediately following the ceremony; feel free to enjoy tasty beverages and snacks while we all mingle and take in the cool city views. Dinner is served at 7pm. Then the real party begins- bring your dancing shoes! Parking is provided by the Downtown Market but is also used by market patrons so space is limited. Please carpool if you're able, if not, street parking is also available on Ionia Street if needed. Wedding attire is, well, wedding attire. We think you guys get it, but if you’re wondering what that means- think semiformal. Dresses for ladies and slacks and button-ups for the menfolk.

Recommendations

We love our city and we are excited to show off all the wonderful things that Grand Rapids has to offer!

Arriving on Friday? We encourage you to explore ArtPrize, the world’s largest art competition, held right here in Downtown GR. Art is literally everywhere. Local businesses, restaurants, and outdoor spaces are transformed into ArtPrize venues so grab some frozen yogurt here and take a peek!
